paella

Description

Paella is a classic Spanish dish featuring saffron rice with chorizo, chicken, and seafood.

    Ingredients
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon ground paprika
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
    Steps
  1. Mix olive oil, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper for marinade in a glass bowl.
  2. Add bell pepper and sausage; cook and stir, breaking sausage up with a spatula, for 5 minutes. Add shrimp; cook and stir until shrimp are bright pink on the outside and the meat is opaque, about 2 minutes.
